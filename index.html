About Me

My name is Egle Libby and I am currently getting ready to move to Portland, OR. At the age 24 I was already managing a pharmacy in Vilnius, Lithuania, for a year, and when my husband got a job offer to work in Bangladesh, I decided to use this opportunity to finally follow my passion for web development. So I quit my job, moved to Bangladesh, and used my free time studying on my own and taking various online courses. I loved it. After I moved to the United States I decide to fully immerse myself in developers' world and joined Viking Code School's Immersive program. I am currently in the middle of the course and enjoying every minute of it. I have always been a fast learner and that helps tremendously to keep up with the course. I am learning so much and can't wait to learn more.
